    1. Cook the chicken: Place breasts in a saucepan; cover with water or broth. Lightly salt. Bring just to a boil, then reduce to a gentle simmer. Cover and cook 12‚Äì15 minutes, until 165¬∞F. Cool slightly.
    2. Shred: Pull apart with two forks, or pulse in a food processor in short bursts until fine shreds form (avoid turning it into a paste).

    4. Make dressing: Whisk mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery seed, celery salt, and pepper.

    Special Tips

    • Chill time matters. Chilling overnight allows the chicken celery salad to develop its full flavor.
